As always we are going to start with a list of things we want to be prepared for. Then we will decide what is a good item to have to help in that situation. Since this kit is going to be in my pocket at all times I want to focus on situations where people might be at risk of dying or ending up in the hospital. Plus things that could permenently scar someone. When building your pocket kit think about the most important things you would could possibly deal with in your life and make sure they are part of the list of things you want to be prepared for.

If you look at everything that is on a person designed to make them ready for the day the list is much larger. This is what that looks like for me.

protection from the elements
proper clothing for the day including footwear, Jacket, umbrella
ability to contact emergency services
Celphone well charged with charger in known location near by
ability to get into my house and my car
Keys and backup cardkey for car if phone key is broken
ability to deal with my personal medical problems
personal medication and 2oz of water in stainless steel flask if I might be away from my water bottle
Treat chronic dry cough for personal comfort and to not bother others
breath mints and a cough drop if it gets bad
ability to fix small things
small pocket-knife with screwdriver and scissors
Cellphone
Credit card
Keys
Cash

All of this is what a lot of people call Every day carry. Every day carry is what you have on you almost every day of your life. A lot of people will first talk about self defence or firstaid. Those are important but as you can see above I like to start with the things every person probably has on them. Like a phone, Keys, watch, currency either a credit card or cash, and your personal medications.
